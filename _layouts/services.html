Projects

  • Idu-Mishmi dictionary app

    An online and offline Idu-Mishmi dictionary (in Roman script) was launched which is available for download here

  • Speech data with transcription + translation (on-going project)

    We are collecting speech data (5 hours per language) along with their transcription and translations in eight languages: Nyishi, Wancho, Galo, Tagin, Kaman, Taraon, Idu and Tangsa. These are the languages selected by the State Counsil of Educational Research and Training (SCERT) to be taught in schools as a 3rd language starting from 2022. The goal of this project is to create free and open-source digital content in these languages, which will serve as language resource for the community and can also help in building language technology tools.

  • Story translation in Idu-Mishmi

    In collaboartion with Microsoft Research India and Pratham books we have tanslated 60 children's stories from English to Idu-Mishmi. These stories are available in Pratham book's Storyweaver platform.
